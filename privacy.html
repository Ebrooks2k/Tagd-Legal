Tagd — Privacy Policy Welcome to Tagd. Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we store it, and what choices you have. By using Tagd, you agree to the terms described below. 1. Information We Collect We collect information in the following categories: 1.1 Account Information When you sign up, we collect: Name Email address Username Profile photo (if applicable) Social platform identifiers (e.g., TikTok open_id, Instagram user ID) 1.2 Connected Social Accounts When you connect your TikTok, Instagram, or other platforms, we receive the permissions you authorize, such as: Basic profile information Content metadata Audience metrics Analytics (views, likes, comments, reach, demographics where allowed) Publishing permissions (if you grant them) We do not access or store your direct messages. 1.3 Content & Media Files If you upload or schedule content: Videos, images, thumbnails Captions, hashtags, metadata Scheduled posting times Draft content 1.4 Analytics & Performance Data To provide insights and growth recommendations, we collect: Engagement metrics (views, likes, comments, saves) Posting times and frequency Audience activity data Platform signals and historical performance 1.5 AI-Derived Data To power recommendations and features, our systems may generate: AI embeddings/vectors Engagement predictions Suggested content themes Forecasted analytics We store these embeddings securely in our database. 1.6 Payment Information Payments are processed securely through Stripe. We do not store or process your credit card number, banking details, or billing credentials directly. 2. How We Use Your Information We use the information we collect to: 2.1 Provide and Improve the App Authenticate your account Fetch analytics from connected platforms Generate growth insights, benchmarks, and recommendations Deliver AI-powered suggestions, scoring, and predictions 2.2 Social Platform Features With your permission, Tagd allows you to: Publish content Auto-schedule and post content Delete posts Manage drafts Access your connected account’s analytics We only perform these actions when you explicitly request them (e.g., scheduling or publishing a post). 2.3 AI Processing Our AI systems may process your: Content Captions Analytics Audience behavior Training is done only on anonymized and aggregated data. We never train models on your identifiable personal content. 2.4 Communication We may send you: Account-related notifications Security alerts Product updates Emails about new features Support messages You may opt out of non-essential communication. 3. How We Share Information We do not sell or share your data with third-party advertisers, data brokers, or marketers. We only share data with: 3.1 Service Providers Vendors who help us operate the platform (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics, database providers). These providers are contractually obligated to: Keep your data secure Only process data on our behalf 3.2 Social Platforms (via API) Only when necessary to: Authenticate your account Post or manage content you’ve scheduled Retrieve analytics or audience data We follow each platform’s API policies strictly. 4. Data Storage & Security 4.1 Storage Location Your data is stored on secure servers hosted by Supabase (United States). 4.2 Security Measures We use: Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) Secure API access Access logging Role-based access controls Least-privilege principles Despite our efforts, no system is 100% secure. If a breach occurs, we will notify affected users promptly. 5. Your Rights & Choices 5.1 Disconnect Social Accounts You may disconnect your TikTok, Instagram, or other platforms at any time from inside the app. Disconnecting immediately stops all data syncing. 5.2 Access & Export You may request a copy of the data we store about you. 5.3 Delete Your Data We provide full deletion upon request. If you delete your account: All stored content AI embeddings Analytics Tokens Scheduling data …will be permanently removed, except where retention is required by law. 5.4 Withdraw Permissions You can revoke platform permissions (e.g., TikTok, Instagram) through the platform’s own settings at any time. 6. Retention We retain your data for as long as your account remains active and delete it when: You request deletion, or Your account becomes inactive for a period defined by our internal policies AI embeddings are deleted when your underlying data is deleted. 7. Children’s Privacy Tagd is not intended for individuals under 13 (or the minimum age allowed in your region). We do not knowingly collect information from minors. 8. International Users If you access Tagd from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in the U.S., where our servers are located. 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this policy occasionally. If material changes occur, we will provide notice through email or in-app alerts. 10.
